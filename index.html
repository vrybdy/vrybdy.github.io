Step 1: In a large mixing bowl stir the flour, salt, baking powder and sugar.

Step 2: Add the milk and egg and stir until there are no lumps.

Step 3: Grease a pan with butter or oil. put in a 1/4 cup of batter in the pan.

Step 4 Flip the pancake bubbles start forming on top of the pancake. Wait for 40 seconds before putting on a plate.

Pizza

  • Time: 30mins
  • Serves: 6
    • Ingredients Quantity
    medium pizza base
    tomato paste
    grated mozzarella
    bacon pieces
    pineapple chunks
    		 1
    1/4 cup
    2/3 cup
    1/3 cup
    1 cup
    400g

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 210'C (200 fan forced). Put the pizza base in a bowl and cover it whith cling wrap. Leave it in the fridge for an hour.

Step 2: Drain the pineapple chunks and put them in a bowl and drain again.

Step 3: Roll the pizza base on a greased baking tray to a 30cm legnth and width . Spread the tomato paste on the pizza base, then top with the pineapple chunks.

Step 4: Put half of the cheese and bacon on the pizza, then add the rest of the cheese and bacon.

Step 5 Bake for 25 minutes or until crust is hard.
